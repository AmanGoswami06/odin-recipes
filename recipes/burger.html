Burger Recipe

This Cast Iron Skillet Pizza recipe is easy and fun to make. It bakes in the oven in about 15 minutes. I top it with pepperoni in this recipe, but you can use your favorite toppings to make restaurant-style pizza in your own kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 pounds (21 ounces) cold ground beef (80 percent lean)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons fine salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 sesame burger buns
  • 4 to 8 slices American cheese, optional
  • Optional toppings: mayonnaise, leaf lettuce, sliced tomato, sliced onion, pickles
